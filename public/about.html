About USA Tours

USA Tours is a tour company that offers different trips throughout America allowing people to experience different
cities and sightsThe purpose of this website is to allow a user to organize information about a tour company that
offers tours throughout the United States. There is information pertaining to the tour directors and tourists. Each
person has a first and last name, address, phone number, and social security number. The tourists each have credit
card payment information. Other information that can be accessed is the the tour options made available through the
tour company that people can sign up for. These tours have a name, a main state or location, number of days, and
the trip's cost. Last but not least, the tour company has contracts or partnerships with other businesses such as
airlines, hotels, and restaurants which helps bring the cost down for each tour offered.
The user is able to add, edit, and delete information in the database as they feel necessary.
